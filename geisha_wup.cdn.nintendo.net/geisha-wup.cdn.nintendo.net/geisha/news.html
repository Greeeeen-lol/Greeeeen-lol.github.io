Welcome to the Custom Games Portal!

Dear Developer,

Welcome to your custom homebrew and templates offline launcher portal! This app is configured to act as a local sandbox template directory for all your custom free games.

Key Features:
- Completely offline: No connection to Nintendo servers is required.
- Local games database: Dynamically loaded from js/database.js.
- Simulation support: Click "Free Download" to simulate a local download and installation. Once complete, start games directly.
- Fully English UI localization.

Enjoy customizing your launcher and coding new games!
