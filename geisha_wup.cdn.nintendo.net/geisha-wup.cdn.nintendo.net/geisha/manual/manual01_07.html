Watching Videos

Select videos on the software details page or other screens to watch clips related to the software.

1

2

3

4

1Pause / Play Button

Touch to pause video playback. Touch again to resume.

2Playback Bar

Displays the current playback position. Slide to rewind or fast forward.

3Fit Screen Button

Touch to fit the video to the height or width of your TV screen. Touch again to revert.

4Options

Displays the video title and related clips. You can change video quality or touch "View Details" to go to the software details page.

  • ※Depending on your internet connection, video or audio playback may stutter.
  • ※Videos cannot be saved to the console's system memory, USB storage devices, or SD cards.

Operating with Buttons and Control Sticks

You can also control video playback using buttons and control sticks on the Wii U GamePad.
