Q&A

●Software Purchases

Q

How do I pay for my purchases?

A

Payments are deducted from your balance, which can be charged using prepaid cards, credit cards, or electronic money.

Q

How do I use coupons?

A

If you have a coupon code from a promotion, select "Use Coupon" on the screen after touching "Proceed to Purchase Screen" and follow the instructions.

Q

Which prepaid cards and codes can be used to add funds?

A

The following can be used:

  • Nintendo Prepaid Card
  • Nintendo Prepaid Code

The following cannot be used:

  • Wii Point Card
  • Wii Point Code
  • Nintendo Point Card
  • Nintendo Point Code

※See "How to Obtain Download Codes" on the Add Funds screen for more details. 8

Q

Where can I buy Nintendo Prepaid Cards?

A

You can purchase them at Nintendo retailers or select convenience stores.

Q

How do I use a Nintendo Prepaid Card?

A

Scratch off the silver panel on the back of the card and enter the 16-character alphanumeric code. 8

Q

Where can I buy Nintendo Prepaid Codes?

A

You can purchase them at convenience store kiosks or cash registers. Check the official Nintendo website for supported chains.

Q

What credit cards are supported?

A

VISA, MasterCard, and JCB.

Q

Which electronic money cards are supported?

A

The following cards can be used (as of July 22, 2014):

  • Kitaca
  • PASMO
  • Suica
  • manaca
  • TOICA
  • ICOCA
  • Hayakaken
  • nimoca
  • SUGOCA

Q

Can I add funds to nfc cards like Suica or PASMO on the Wii U console?

A

No. You cannot add funds to the card itself on the Wii U. Check the electronic money card issuer's website for funding instructions.

Q

Can I buy software using Wii Points?

A

No. Wii Points can only be used in the Wii Shop Channel.

Q

Can I use my Nintendo 3DS eShop balance?

A

Yes, if you register the same Nintendo Network ID on both your Wii U console and Nintendo 3DS system.

Q

Where can find codes issued during a purchase?

A

You can find them on your receipt. Go to My Menu -> Account Activity -> View Details. Look for the "Issue Number" item.

Q

Can I view my Nintendo 3DS account activity?

A

Yes. However, Nintendo 3DS activity from before the ID was registered will not be displayed on Wii U.

●About Software

Q

Are manuals available for software purchased on Nintendo eShop?

A

Yes. You can access the Electronic Manual by pressing the HOME Button while the software is running and selecting "Manual."

Q

Does redownloading software cost money?

A

No, it is free of charge (unless your Nintendo Network ID is deleted). Note that software distribution may be suspended or terminated without notice.

●Data Management

Q

How long does it take to download purchased software?

A

Download times depend on the size of the software and your internet connection speed.

You can check download progress in Download Management on the HOME Button Menu.

Q

Where is purchased software saved?

A

It is saved to the system memory or a USB storage device. It cannot be saved to an SD Card.

Q

Can I copy or move data between system memory and a USB storage device?

A

Yes. See Data Management under System Settings on the Wii U Menu for details.

●Support

Q

Who should I contact for support?

A

It depends on the nature of your inquiry. Go to My Menu -> Help -> Support / Contact Information. 4
