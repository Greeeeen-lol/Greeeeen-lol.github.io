Viewing Charts

Touch "Charts" on the home screen to view charts of best-selling software, user-recommended titles, or popular videos.

1

2

1 Select Chart Type

Best-sellers and popular videos charts are based on activity from the past two weeks. User recommendations charts cover the past 12 weeks.

※See "About Data Collection" for details on chart statistics.
14

※If titles tie, the newer release is listed first.

2 Filter Charts

You can filter charts by software category.
