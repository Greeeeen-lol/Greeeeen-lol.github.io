What is Nintendo eShop?

Nintendo eShop is an online store where you can purchase downloadable Wii U software, add-on content, and more. You can also view detailed information and videos of software, or download demo versions.

  • ※To purchase software, you must first add funds to your balance. 8
  • ※If you register the same Nintendo Network ID on both a Wii U console and a Nintendo 3DS system, you can share your balance and account activity.

Purchasable Software

●Wii U Download Software

This includes download-only software for Wii U and download versions of packaged Wii U software.

●Virtual Console

These are classic games originally released on older consoles such as NES and Super NES.

Virtual Console Special Offer

If you already own the Wii version of a Virtual Console title (either by transferring it from a Wii console, or by purchasing it on the Wii Shop Channel on the Wii Menu), you can purchase the Wii U version of the same title at a special discount price. Please visit the official Nintendo website for more details.
