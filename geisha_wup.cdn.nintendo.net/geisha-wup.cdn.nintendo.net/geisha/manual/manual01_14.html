About Data Collection

User Ratings and video view counts are calculated based on specific criteria.

●User Ratings

The score is the average of recommendations, displayed in half-star increments.

※Ratings are reflected starting from the first vote.

※To calculate a more accurate average, the top 5% and bottom 5% of ratings are excluded.

●Software Charts

Software charts aggregate votes and list titles with 20 or more recommendations, sorted by score.

●Video Charts

Video charts aggregate the number of times clips were viewed, sorted by view count.

Update Frequency

Data is compiled and updated every three hours (updates are not reflected immediately).

※Updates may be delayed due to system processing requirements.
