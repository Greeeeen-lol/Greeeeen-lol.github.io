Purchasing Add-on Content / Passes

Select and purchase "Add-on Content" or "Passes" from the software details page 9.

Purchasing Add-on Content

Available add-on content can be purchased if it has been released.

●Search Content

Search and filter available add-on content by keyword, genre, or price.

※Filtering by genre might not be supported for some add-on content.

●Add-on Content List

Touch to open details for specific add-on content.

●Checkboxes

Touch the checkbox next to the content you want to select it . Touch "View Selected Items" and follow the instructions to purchase.

  • ※Add-on content types and prices vary by software.
  • ※Receipts for add-on content purchases can be viewed in Account Activity on My Menu. 4
  • ※All sales of add-on content are final (no refunds, returns, or exchanges).
  • ※Purchased add-on content can be redownloaded for free even if deleted.
  • ※You cannot redownload content if your Nintendo Network ID is deleted.
  • ※Redownload services may be suspended or terminated without prior notice.
  • ※Purchased add-on content is saved in the same location as the parent software.
  • ※Add-on content is accessible to all users registered on the console.

Purchasing Passes

Passes can be purchased if the software provides subscription-based services.

Passes have an expiration date and take effect immediately upon purchase. If you buy a pass before your current one expires, the new duration will be added to the remaining time.

  • ※Pass durations and prices vary by software.
  • ※Subscriptions do not auto-renew automatically even if you have enough balance. Please purchase a new pass when the current one expires.
  • ※Pass purchase records and start dates can be viewed in Account Activity on My Menu. 4
  • ※All sales of passes are final (no refunds, returns, or exchanges).
  • ※Active passes are accessible to all users registered on the console.
  • ※To buy an additional pass while one is active, you must use the same user account that made the original purchase.
