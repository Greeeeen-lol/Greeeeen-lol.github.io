Adding Funds

Touch "Add Funds" on the menu bar to open the fund adding screen.

※Funds are managed separately for each user.

To add funds (charge your balance), you need one of the following:

  • Nintendo Prepaid Card
  • Nintendo Prepaid Code
  • Credit Card
  • Prepaid Electronic Money (Suica, PASMO, etc.)

※Prepaid cards and added funds cannot be used in the Wii Shop Channel.

※If you registered the same Nintendo Network ID on both your Wii U console and Nintendo 3DS system, your balance will be shared with the Nintendo 3DS eShop.

Add Funds using a Nintendo Prepaid Code

Select this option to use a Nintendo Prepaid Card or Prepaid Code. You can also view details on how to purchase prepaid codes.

Add Funds using a Credit Card

Select this option to use a credit card. Receipts can be viewed in your Account Activity on My Menu. 4
You can save credit card details to add funds in the future simply by entering your password.

※Your saved credit card details will be deleted if you enter the password incorrectly three times.

※If you forget your password, delete the credit card information and register it again.

Confirm Credit Card Info

Verify or delete saved credit card information.

※Users under 18 cannot use credit cards unless parents or guardians give consent. Please follow the on-screen instructions.

Use Electronic Money

Select this option to add funds using prepaid electronic money card. Receipts can be viewed in Account Activity. 4 Follow the on-screen instructions and touch your card to the NFC area mark on the Wii U GamePad.

※Touch the card firmly to the NFC area mark as guided. If the card is too far or touched too briefly, it might not be read correctly.
