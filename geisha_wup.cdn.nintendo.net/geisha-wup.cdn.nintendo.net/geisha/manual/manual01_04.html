●Adding Funds8

Wish List

Displays titles added to your Wish List.

Touch a software icon to go to its details page. 6

Touch the "View List" button to display all titles registered on your Wish List.

※You can add up to 200 titles to your Wish List.

User Recommendations

Displays titles you can recommend.

Touch a software icon and answer three simple questions to recommend a title you've played to others.

Touch the "View List" button to display all titles you can recommend.

The voting results are compiled and displayed on software details pages and in the charts. 6 12

※Each user can recommend a title only once, and only after playing it for at least one hour.

※You cannot recommend a title if your age is lower than the software rating.

※Some software cannot be recommended.

※You can view your recommendation history in your Account Activity.

History

●Redownloadable Software

View and redownload previously purchased software or add-on content.

●Account Activity

Displays records of purchased software and added funds. Touch "View Details" to view receipts.

※If you register the same Nintendo Network ID on both a Wii U console and a Nintendo 3DS system, your account activity will be shared. However, Nintendo 3DS activity from before the ID was registered will not be displayed on Wii U.

Settings

●Notification Settings

Choose whether to receive notifications from Nintendo eShop on your Wii U Menu.

●Language Settings

Change the display language for Nintendo eShop. You must close and restart Nintendo eShop for the language change to take effect.

※The language setting applies only to Nintendo eShop.

●Location Settings

Register, modify, or delete your location information.

※Location information is used to calculate tax when purchasing software.

※The configured location information applies only to Nintendo eShop.
