Purchasing Software

Touch "Proceed to Purchase Screen" on the details page 6 or search results 5 to proceed. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the purchase.

  • ※Software download starts automatically after purchase. Check progress in Download Management on the HOME Button Menu.
  • ※Purchased software can be played by all users registered on the console.
